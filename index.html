About Us

Foundation Scott is the sponsor of many studios, such as Juicify Studios and Scott Productions. It serves as the main studio and sponsor of all branches. Its main purpose is to hold all games we make. Scott Productions is a Roblox branch of Foundation Scott used only for Roblox games, while Foundation Scott is used for all types, including Python games, Java games, and more.

Scott RPG

Scott RPG is a Windows command prompt-based text RPG where the player can explore dungeons, find treasure, fight monsters, and choose different character classes.
